COCA‑COLA NIGERIA CHAMPIONS
CALL FOR A UNITED NIGERIA
The Africa Cup of Nations is a unifying game in the continent. It is a game that brings together nations within Africa and a strong platform for unity. As part of the strategic thrust of brand Coca‑Cola to unite people and forester togetherness, Coca‑Cola developed African Cup of Nations (AFCON) 2019 campaign to support the Nigeria National Football Team in an Iconic communication theme “Nigeria United”, a movement created to inspire unity and patriotism across age, class, tribe and gender.

Building on Coca‑Cola timeless support for the beautiful game, Nigeria United fortified Coca‑Cola’s commitment to supporting football locally and globally and helped drive wide support for our treasured Super Eagles.

To amplify this message of unity and patriotism, Coca Cola recently released a new ad calling every Nigerian, status or station notwithstanding, to contribute their part in bringing our nation together as one.

Speaking at the unveiling, Gbolahan Sanni, Franchise Marketing Manager explained that: “Nigeria United campaign is a mobilization call to all Nigerians to passionately support our Super Eagles during the 2019 Africa Cup of Nations. But more, it is a call for each one of us as patriots, to actively play a part in building the Nigeria of our dreams”.

Coca‑Cola believes in the potential of this nation; hence, has been supporting Nigerians in diverse ways.

He further stated that the campaign is designed to further reinforce Coca‑Cola’s support for Nigeria and the Nigerian. The brand believes we can achieve the Nigeria of our dream if we play our parts and work together.
